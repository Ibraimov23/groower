About

An upcoming web3
game and a farming
simulator

Groowers tells a story about sloths who love to grow and
smokeweed, while cruising through galaxies on their spaceship. The
gameplay includes farming crops, improving breeds, trading, social
events, and much more.

Not only limited to
weed enthusiasts,

the Groowers game brings together all people around ideas of
peace, friendship, and prosperity. Join the game to earn some
money or simply hang out with cool people in our community.

How to PLAY

0/1

seed

Start your farming career by buying a
seed. Each stain has its own unique
genetics - choose wisely!

0/2

pod

Put the seed in a pod to start the
process. Be careful - pods have limited
durability and differ in their ability to
keep the optimal conditions for the
growth.

0/3

grow

Take care of your plants and patiently
wait until they mature. Surprise -
harvesting crops on different stages will
bring you different results!

0/4

earn

Sell your BUDS harvest to earn some
money or reinvest profits to become an
even more efficient Groower.
